Coating Testing

St. Louis Testing Laboratories' coating tests utilize capabilities from all of our departments to bring you a fast, accurate, and professional testing for all your coating needs.

  • Coefficient of Friction, Thickness, SEM Analysis
  • Abrasion Testing – Taber Abrasion, Chip Resistance, Peel Testing
  • Chemical Resistance – Salt Spray, Cure Testing, Chemical Analysis, Contamination Analysis
  • Coating Hardness – Rockwell, Brinell, Shore
  • Cathodic Disbondment Testing
  • Military Coating Certification
  • Dielectric Breakdown Testing

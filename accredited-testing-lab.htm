Scope of Accreditation



St. Louis Testing Laboratories is accredited by The American Association for Laboratory Accreditation (A2LA), a leading nonprofit, industry association that provides accreditation of testing laboratories, calibration laboratories, inspection bodies, proficiency testing providers, medical testing laboratories, reference material producers and product certification bodies. We are also certified by the United States Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC).
